Our Vision

To be the innovator in providing globally-competitive education and academic excellence in the field of arts and sciences; responsive to the demands of the industrial revolution and contemporary world.

Our Mission

  • To establish linkages with leading academic and industry experts.
  • To achieve the highest international level of excellence through high-impact quality research, development & publication.
  • To provide an environment that fosters the welfare and development of the stakeholders.
  • To nurture a community of successful and socially-responsible learners and leaders.
  • To provide accessible and cost-effective education for all.

Goals and Objectives

To employ highly-qualified teaching personnel and research faculty.

To facilitate industry-based faculty development programs.

To develop up-to-date curricula and course materials.

To cater quality student services.

To enjoin community outreach/extension programs.

To provide state-of-the-art facilities.

To improve the quality of living of the stakeholders.
